Baluni Public School, Tilwari

Baluni Public School, Tilwari (BPS) is a prominent school educating young prodigies. Baluni Public School commenced its first session on 2nd April 2012 for classes Nursery to VII. In session 2013 the school will start class IX and the same students will appear for class X CBSE exam in 2014. In its further stage the school will start class XI in 2015 and the same students will appear for class XII CBSE exam in 2016.

Baluni Group of Education was conceived in the year 2002 by eminent personality Dr. Navin Baluni, a medico from S.N. Medical College, Agra to spread unique education system & facility especially in Dehradun, Agra and other parts of North India.
